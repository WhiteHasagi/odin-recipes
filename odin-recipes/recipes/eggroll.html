How to make Egg Rolls

A basic type of food that is not very complicated but still required some mediocre level of cooking skill.

Ingredients:

  • Ground pork
  • Shredded cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Sesame oil

Steps:
  1. Cut down the ingredients to smaller pieces
  2. Fill the cutted ingredients into the egg roll wrappers
  3. Fried the egg rolls in oil at around 350 degrees in few minutes
  4. Add sauce to dip in (optional)
